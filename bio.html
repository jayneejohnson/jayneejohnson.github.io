Biography

I am originally from Fort Pierce, Fla. I now reside in the Swamp with my little sister and Doxie, Ty.

My father is a fourth-generation citrus farmer and cattle rancher. My agricultural roots have helped shape me the person I am today. I have the most loving parents and two little sisters. I am the oldest, and this responsibility has also contributed to the person I am today.

My favorite things include: family, fashion, travel, being a foodie, public relations, writing, holidays and event planning, especially weddings.

If I could incorporate public relations and event planning into one, I would have my dream job.
