Biography

I am originally from Fort Pierce, Fla. I now reside in the Swamp with my little sister and Doxie, Ty.

My father is a fourth-generation citrus farmer and cattle rancher. My agricultural roots have helped shape me into the person I am today. I have the most loving, supportive parents and sisters. I am the oldest of three girls, and this responsibility has also contributed to the person I am today.

My favorite things include: family, fashion, travel, being a foodie, public relations, writing, blogging, organizing, exercising, holidays and event planning, especially weddings.

My dream job would be to incorporate public relations and event planning into one.
